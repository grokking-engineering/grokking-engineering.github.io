Grokking TechTalks

Grokking TechTalks is our regular, flagship event series, where we invite experienced engineers / speakers working on various fields in Computer Science and Software Engineering to to share about their experience and knowledge.

Our talks are highly technical and target working software engineers. Each talk usually attract an audience of 50-100 people. The talk is organized once every 6-8 weeks.

If you/your company is interested in giving talks, contact us techtalks@grokking.org and we will get back to you with more details.
